CT Scan (Computed Tomography)

Our CT scan services provide detailed cross-sectional images of your body, allowing for precise diagnosis of various conditions. We use advanced CT technology for comprehensive evaluation.

  • Brain & Head CT Scan
  • Chest CT Scan
  • Abdominal & Pelvic CT
  • Spine CT Scan
  • CT Angiography
  • CT Guided Procedures
  • Contrast & Non-contrast Studies

MRI provides detailed images of organs, soft tissues, bones, and internal structures without using radiation. Our advanced MRI services offer superior diagnostic capabilities.

  • Brain & Neurological MRI
  • Spinal MRI
  • Musculoskeletal MRI (Joints, Muscles)
  • Abdominal MRI
  • Pelvic MRI
  • Cardiac MRI
  • MR Angiography

Advantages: No radiation exposure, excellent soft tissue contrast, detailed visualization, and non-invasive imaging technique.

ECG / EKG Services

Electrocardiogram (ECG) testing to assess your heart's electrical activity and detect cardiac conditions. Quick, painless, and non-invasive procedure.

  • Resting ECG
  • Exercise Stress Test
  • 24-Hour Holter Monitoring
