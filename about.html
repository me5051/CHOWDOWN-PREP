About REENE Medical Diagnostic Center

Excellence in Medical Diagnostics Since Our Establishment

Who We Are

REENE Medical Diagnostic Center is a leading medical diagnostic facility located in the heart of Awada, Onitsha North, Anambra State. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive, accurate, and timely diagnostic services to individuals and healthcare providers across Onitsha and surrounding communities.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with modern diagnostic equipment operated by highly trained medical professionals who are committed to excellence in patient care. We understand that accurate diagnosis is the foundation of effective treatment, which is why we invest in the latest technology and continuous staff training.

With years of experience serving the Anambra State community, we have built a reputation for reliability, professionalism, and compassionate care. Our mission is to make quality diagnostic services accessible and affordable to everyone who needs them.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide accurate, reliable, and timely medical diagnostic services using state-of-the-art technology while maintaining the highest standards of patient care and professionalism.

Vision: To be the most trusted and preferred medical diagnostic center in Anambra State and the South-East region of Nigeria, recognized for excellence in service delivery and patient satisfaction.
